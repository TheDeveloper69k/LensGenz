Why we exist

A magazine that publishes its readers, not just its editors.

Most magazines are a one-way conversation. LensGenz flips that: anyone can submit an article, a photo gallery, or a full PDF issue. Our small editorial team reviews every submission, and once it's approved, it's published for the whole community to read.

How it works

Three steps to getting published

01

Write or upload

Draft an article with our built-in editor, upload a photo series, or attach a full PDF issue.

02

Editors review

Every submission goes into a review queue. We check for quality and fit before anything goes live.

03

Get published

Approved work appears on Explore and the homepage, credited to you, for the whole community to enjoy.
